# Ionnetix Technologies — Design System (Final + Motion)

---

## 1. Design Philosophy

* Modern startup aesthetic
* Clean, minimal, and conversion-focused
* Built for clarity, not decoration
* Motion enhances UX — never distracts from content

---

## 2. Color System

### Primary (Trust & Technology)

* Deep Navy Blue: `#0F2C59`
* Usage:

  * Headings
  * Primary sections
  * Dark backgrounds

---

### Accent (Action & Energy)

* Bright Cyan: `#00D2FF`
* Usage:

  * CTA buttons
  * Highlights
  * Hover states

---

### Background

* White: `#FFFFFF`
* Light Gray: `#F8FAFC`

---

### Text Colors

* Primary Text: `#0F172A`
* Secondary Text: `#334155`

---

## 3. Typography

### Headings

* Font: **Plus Jakarta Sans**
* Bold, modern, high contrast

### Body

* Font: **Inter**
* Clean and readable

### Rules

* Max 2–3 lines per paragraph
* Avoid dense text blocks

---

## 4. Spacing & Layout System

### Container

* Max Width: `1200px`
* Center aligned

### Section Spacing

* Desktop: `80px – 120px`
* Mobile: `48px – 64px`

### Grid

* 12-column system

---

## 5. Component Design Rules

### Buttons

* Border radius: `8px – 12px`
* Consistent padding

#### Primary CTA

* Background: Cyan
* Hover: Slightly darker cyan

#### Secondary

* Outline or Navy

---

### Cards

* Light shadow
* Subtle border
* Clean spacing

---

### Inputs

* Minimal
* Cyan focus state

---

## 6. Iconography & Visuals

* Minimal, outline-based
* Neutral colors + cyan highlights
* No inconsistent icon sets

---

## 7. Logo Usage

* Maintain spacing
* No distortion
* White version for dark backgrounds

---

## 8. Visual Hierarchy

* H1: Dominant
* H2: Sections
* Body: Short, scannable

---

## 9. UX Rules

* No unnecessary clicks
* Key info above the fold
* Clear navigation

---

## 10. Motion & Scroll Animation System (Controlled)

### Core Principle

Animations must:

* Improve readability
* Guide attention
* Never delay important content

---

### Allowed Animation Types

#### 1. Fade + Up (Primary)

* Opacity: 0 → 1
* Y: 30–40px → 0
* Duration: 0.4 – 0.6s

---

#### 2. Subtle Scale

* Scale: 0.95 → 1
* Used for cards and images only

---

#### 3. Stagger Animation

* Delay between items: 0.1 – 0.2s
* Used in:

  * Service cards
  * Process steps

---

### Where to Use Animations

#### Use In:

* Hero section (light entrance)
* Section reveals
* Service cards
* Process steps
* Final CTA highlight

---

#### Avoid In:

* Navbar
* Forms and inputs
* Long paragraphs
* Footer
* Critical CTA buttons (must always be instantly visible)

---

### Interaction Animations

#### Hover Effects:

* Buttons: slight brightness or scale (max 1.05)
* Cards: subtle elevation

---

### Performance Rules

* Animations run **once only**
* Do not re-trigger on scroll
* Limit number of animated elements per section
* Must work smoothly on low-end devices

---

### Accessibility Rule

* Content must be visible even if animations fail
* No hidden content dependent on JS

---

## 11. Conversion Design Rules (Critical)

### Every Page Must:

* CTA above the fold
