# Ionnetix Technologies — Website Plan

## 1. Objective

Build a lightweight, high-conversion website that:

* Generates leads (WhatsApp, calls, email)
* Establishes credibility despite limited project history
* Clearly communicates services and business value

---

## 2. Target Audience

* Local businesses (retail, gyms, service providers)
* Small startups / founders
* Non-technical business owners

---

## 3. Core Positioning

"We help small businesses get more customers and automate operations using custom technology and AI."

---

## 4. Key Goals

* Drive inquiries via WhatsApp and contact forms
* Educate users about solutions (not just services)
* Build trust without relying heavily on past projects

---

## 5. Website Structure

### Pages:

1. Home
2. Services
3. Projects (In Progress / Coming Soon)
4. About
5. Contact

---

## 6. Page Breakdown

### Home

* Hero (clear value proposition + CTA)
* Problem statements (pain points)
* Solutions overview
* Services preview
* Process section
* Trust indicators
* Strong CTA (WhatsApp + Call)

---

### Services

Each service structured as:

* Problem
* Solution
* Outcome

Services:

* Web Development (lead generation websites)
* Digital Marketing (customer acquisition)
* AI Automation (workflow optimization)

---

### Projects

* “In Progress” projects
* Concept showcases
* Problem → Solution → Expected impact

---

### About

* Company focus
* Approach to problem-solving
* Technical mindset

---

### Contact

* WhatsApp integration (primary)
* Contact form (short)
* Email + phone

---

## 7. Features

* Sticky WhatsApp button
* Fast-loading pages
* Mobile-first design
* SEO basics (meta tags, headings)
* Simple form handling

---

## 8. Tech Stack

* Next.js (frontend)
* Tailwind CSS (styling)
* Vercel (deployment)
* Email API / Form handler

---

## 9. Timeline (Realistic)

* Planning: 1–2 days
* Design: 2–3 days
* Development: 5–7 days
* Testing & deploy: 1–2 days

---

## 10. Future Enhancements

* Case studies with real data
* Blog / insights section
* Demo products showcase
* CRM integration
